about us

Jobkey University (JU) is a premier non-profit higher education institution established to serve the Somali society through quality teaching, research, and community development. As a center of academic excellence, JU fosters intellectual growth, ethical leadership, and innovation-driven education that aligns with Somalia’s national development agenda and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 4 – Quality Education).
Vision
To be among the leading universities in the Horn of Africa, recognized for academic excellence, student success, research innovation, and societal impact that drive sustainable development and global competitiveness.
Mission
To cultivate and enrich individual potential and societal advancement through quality teaching, transformative learning, applied research, and professional practice in an inclusive and supportive environment that promotes intellectual growth, faith, and culture.

Core Objectives

To respond to the needs of society by providing accessible, high-quality education that develops competent and skilled professionals. To offer free community training programs that foster capacity development and lifelong learning. To promote research, innovation, and technology integration across all disciplines. To enhance international partnerships and knowledge exchange for global relevance.
